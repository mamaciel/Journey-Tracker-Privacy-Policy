Privacy Policy

For: Journey Tracker Photo Journal

Information We Collect

Journey Tracker collects and stores the following information to provide its services:

  • Photos you choose to upload
  • Photo metadata (dates, titles, and descriptions you provide)
  • User account information (email and display name)
  • Optional profile pictures

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Store and organize your photo collections
  • Provide access to your photos across devices
  • Manage your account and preferences
  • Improve our services and user experience

Data Storage

Your photos and data are securely stored using:

  • Amazon S3 for photo storage
  • Firebase for user authentication and metadata
  • Local device caching for improved performance

Photo Library Access

Journey Tracker requests access to your device's photo library only when you choose to:

  • Import photos to your collections
  • Save photos from collections to your device
  • Set profile or collection avatar pictures

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data and photos. Your content is only accessible through your authenticated account.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at: thejourneytrackerapp@gmail.com
